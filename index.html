Greater Good Science Center – UC Berkeley

Compassion releases natural chemicals in the brain that reduce stress and improve mood. These effects support heart health, lower blood pressure, and promote emotional balance. Simple daily acts of kindness help create stronger relationships and better physical and mental well-being.
