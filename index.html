This site explores how emotional states like empathy and compassion affect physical health. Scientists are finding links between compassionate behavior and reduced cortisol levels, lower blood pressure, and improved longevity.

Physiological Mechanisms

  • Reduced Stress Response: Compassion activates the parasympathetic nervous system, helping the body relax and recover from stress.
  • Improved Heart Health: Acts of kindness can increase heart rate variability, a marker of good cardiovascular health.
  • Hormonal Balance: Compassion releases oxytocin, which reduces blood pressure and promotes bonding and calmness.
